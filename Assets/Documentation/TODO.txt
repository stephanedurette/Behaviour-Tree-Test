Job
	Progress Value - bound to job view progress bar

	Register Job - finds job manager and makes this job available
	Unregister Job - same thing makes this job unavailable

Behaviour Action FindJob -> returns job, finds an available job from the jobmanager
Behaviour Action WalkToJob -> move to job using pathfinding
Behaviour Action StartJob -> assign unit to job and work the job until it's done

Behaviour Action IsItemBackpackFull -> is the unit full of any resource
