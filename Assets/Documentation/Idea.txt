--- City Game ---

RESOURCES
wood
gold
wheat

NEEDS
food
sleep

JOBS
mine
wood
gold
guard
build

BUILDABLE
ore vien - mining - respawns periodically
tree - wood cutting - respawns periodically
wheat plot - farming - respawns periodically

restaurant - eating
house - sleeping
weapon rack - for defense - units run over to it and come out armed
turret - for defense

guard tower - for keeping guards

barracks - units go in and come out as guards

walls

goblins attack periodically
new workers come in periodically

GAMEPLAY
ui shows jobs and number of units assigned to jobs, units then go do those jobs

UNITS
default - will find job between, mining chopping and farming and do those jobs so long as it doesn't need to eat or sleep
	- when attacked will find a weapon rack and grab a weapon and fight or try to run away

guard - will patrol to least occupied guard towers
	- will attack enemies as they appear
	- will operate turrets when nearby
